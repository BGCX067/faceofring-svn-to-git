App manages your ring and vibration settings depending on phone orientation.

Vibration is off when the phone is lying on the horizontal surface. When you have a meeting, put the phone face down to turn off its ringing. Soft vibration informs you about a call. Turn it to see who is calling. Put it back if you cannot answer

This is a new version 1.2.1 with improved sensor stability in case when the phone has a vibration or a loud ringer volume
